The Power of the Leaf

Yerba Mate is more than just a drink; it's a nutritional powerhouse used for centuries by the Guaraní people.

High in Antioxidants

Yerba Mate contains 90% more antioxidants than green tea. It's packed with polyphenols and saponins that boost the immune system and protect cells.

Natural Energy Boost

Experience a "clean" energy boost. Mate contains a unique balance of caffeine, theobromine, and theophylline, providing focus without the coffee crash.

Nutrient Dense

Contains 24 vitamins and minerals, including Vitamin A, C, E, B1, B2, B3, B5, and B complex, plus calcium, iron, and magnesium.

Supports Metabolism

Studies suggest that Yerba Mate can aid in weight management by increasing fat oxidation and feelings of fullness.

A Natural State of Wellness

With its combination of nutrients and sustained energy, Yerba Mate is the perfect companion for a productive day and a healthy lifestyle.
