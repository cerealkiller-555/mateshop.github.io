Our Story

Born from a passion for energy that doesn't sacrifice health.

Energy, Reimagined

At Sip & Energize, we were tired of the jittery highs and crashing lows of coffee and sugary energy drinks. We discovered Yerba Mate—a traditional South American herb that provided the perfect balance of focus, vitality, and well-being.

Our mission is to bring the highest quality, sustainably sourced Yerba Mate to your cup, so you can power through your day with natural, sustained energy.

Our Commitment

We work directly with growers to ensure every leaf is harvested with respect for the environment and the traditions that make Yerba Mate so special.
