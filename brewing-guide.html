The Art of Brewing Yerba Mate

From traditional gourds to modern infusers, discover the perfect way to prepare your mate.

1. Traditional Gourd & Bombilla

The most authentic way to enjoy Yerba Mate, creating a unique ritual and social connection.

  • Fill: Add Mate leaves to your gourd until about 2/3 full.
  • Tilt: Shake the gourd so the leaves slope to one side.
  • Moisten: Pour a little cool water into the bottom of the "well".
  • Insert: Place your bombilla into the well.
  • Brew: Add hot (not boiling!) water (70°C-80°C) and enjoy.

2. French Press Method

Perfect for those who want the energy boost without the traditional ritual.

  • Add 1-2 tablespoons of Mate per cup.
  • Moisten with cool water first to protect the flavor.
  • Add hot water and let steep for 3-5 minutes.
  • Press slowly and pour.

Pro Tip: Never Use Boiling Water

Boiling water (100°C) will burn the leaves and create a bitter taste. Aim for 75°C (167°F) for the smoothest flavor profile and maximum nutrient retention.
